# llamppost Marketplace Policy (Creator-facing)

This policy explains what you can and cannot publish on llamppost.

## 1. IP / Copyright
- Do not upload content you do not own.
- If your avatar/persona/skill uses any brand, character, movie/anime/game assets, you must provide a valid license.
- "Inspired by" is allowed. Direct copying, tracing, or simple style-transfer of protected works is not.

## 2. Publicity & Portrait Rights
- Do not use a real person's face/likeness without consent.
- Public figures still have rights. If your work resembles a real person, you may be asked for proof of consent.

## 3. Minors Safety (Zero Tolerance)
- No sexual content involving minors.
- If age is ambiguous, we treat it as minor.

## 4. Hate, Harassment, and Violence
- No hate content or harassment.
- No encouragement of self-harm or violence.

## 5. Privacy & Secrets
- Do not include personal data (phone, address, IDs) in your content.
- Do not include API keys, tokens, or passwords in any file.

## 6. Marketplace Integrity
- Do not misrepresent testing.
- Clearly state known limits.

## 7. Enforcement
- We may reject, remove, or suspend listings that violate policy.
- Repeat violations may lead to account restrictions.

## 8. How to Report
- Contact support with links and evidence.
